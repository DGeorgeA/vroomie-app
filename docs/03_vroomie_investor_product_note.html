Technology Moat

A defensible stack combining world-class AI, proprietary data, and frictionless distribution.

🧠

On-Device Machine Learning

All AI inference runs directly on the user's device via TensorFlow.js. No server round-trip, no latency, no privacy concerns. Works fully offline after initial model download.

🎯

Google YAMNet Integration

Built on Google's state-of-the-art audio classification model, pre-trained on millions of audio samples. Extracts 1024-dimensional acoustic embeddings with exceptional accuracy.

🔐

Proprietary Fault Fingerprints

Our curated anomaly fingerprint database uses cosine similarity matching against reference fault signatures. Each new diagnosis improves the model's accuracy — a compounding data moat.

⚡

Sub-Second Classification

Temporal embedding pipeline processes 2-second audio windows with 80-dimensional composite features, delivering results in under one second on mid-range smartphones.

🌐

PWA Distribution

Progressive Web App architecture eliminates app store gatekeeping, reduces distribution friction to near-zero, and enables instant updates across all platforms simultaneously.

📊

Every detected fault is paired with a clinical-grade technical narrative explaining the root cause, risk trajectory, and precise repair steps — building trust and reducing mechanic dependency.

Section 06

Supported Fault Types

Six precision-engineered fault classifiers at launch — each backed by clinical-grade acoustic fingerprints and expert-written diagnostic narratives.

Fault Type Severity Clinical Description
Alternator Bearing Fault
 🔴 Critical The alternator's internal bearing race is failing, generating a sustained high-frequency whine (4–8 kHz). Causes increasing electrical load instability, progressively draining the battery and risking complete loss of vehicle charging while driving.
Bearing Alternator Degradation
 🟠 High Severe alternator bearing degradation with metal-on-metal contact within the bearing races. Unaddressed, this leads to total alternator seizure and a stranded vehicle.
Intake Leak
 🟡 Low A vacuum or air leak in the intake manifold introduces unmetered air into the combustion mixture, causing a lean fuel condition, misfires under load, and rough idling. Prolonged lean running risks piston and valve damage.
Engine Misfire
 🟡 Medium One or more cylinders are failing to complete the combustion cycle reliably. Unburnt fuel enters the exhaust stream, damaging the catalytic converter over time.
Motor Starter Issue
 🟠 High An electrical transient consistent with starter motor engagement or relay chatter has been detected. May indicate a weak battery, corroded terminals, or a failing starter solenoid.
Piston Knock
 🟠 High Deep low-frequency knocking consistent with piston slap or big-end bearing wear, indicating significant internal engine wear. Engine rebuild may be required if clearances exceed specification.

6 fault types at launch — expanding to 20+ by Q4 2026 including timing chain, serpentine belt, power steering, water pump, rocker arm, and valve train anomalies.
